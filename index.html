About Me

I’m a Product designer, who enjoys listening to people’s stories, with a background in healthcare and psychology. I’m driven by people’s stories of their lives, their interactions with technology and each other, and empathy. I’m inspired by the ability for collaboration to solve problems — proving over and over that each individual as well as the larger group are equally important.

When I’m not conducting research or pushing pixels, I’m either ‘blogging’, jogging, or just plain exploring my nearby natural environments and neighborhoods.
